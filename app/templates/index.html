Our Frequently Asked Questions

How does Seed a Founder work?

Simply sign up and submit your pitch deck. Browse through our list of investors and connect with those that align with your requirements. If an investor expresses interest, you can negotiate directly with them, or we can assist in structuring the deal.

What kind of projects does Seed a Founder support?

Seed a Founder supports a diverse array of projects, ranging from technology startups and social enterprises to sustainable initiatives and innovative ventures spanning various industries. The only criteria are that the founders must be based in Africa.

How do I invest through Seed a Founder?

Once your investor profile is complete, you'll gain access to all available projects. From there, you can engage with founders, ask questions, request additional information, and make investments seamlessly.
